Overview

SnugBlox ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

The short version: We don't collect any data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Data Collection

SnugBlox does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data. Specifically:

  • No account or login required
  • No personal information collected
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or ad networks
  • No network requests or internet connection required

Local Storage

The app stores only your high score locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is deleted if you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

SnugBlox does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms. We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Children's Privacy

SnugBlox does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.
