Overview

SnugBlox ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

The short version: We don't collect any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Data Collection

SnugBlox does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data. Specifically:

  • No account or login required
  • No personal information collected
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or ad networks

Local Storage

The app stores your game progress locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage. This includes:

  • Your high score
  • Saved game state (to continue where you left off)
  • Your settings preferences

This data never leaves your device, is not accessible to us or any third party, and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

Game Center

SnugBlox optionally integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards. If you choose to use Game Center:

  • Your Game Center nickname and scores are managed by Apple
  • We do not have access to your Apple ID or personal information
  • Game Center usage is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
  • You can disable Game Center in your device settings

Third-Party Services

Other than the optional Game Center integration, SnugBlox does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

SnugBlox does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
