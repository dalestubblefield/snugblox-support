SnugBlox

Cozy Block Puzzle

About the Game

SnugBlox is a relaxing block puzzle game designed to feel like a warm blanket for your brain.

Drag and drop colorful block pieces onto the grid. Fill a row or column to clear it and score points. Simple to learn, satisfying to master.

How to Play

  • Drag block pieces from the tray onto the 8x8 grid
  • Fill any complete row or column to clear it
  • Place all three pieces to get a new set
  • Clear 3+ lines at once to earn a shuffle
  • Use shuffles when stuck to get new pieces
  • Keep going until no pieces fit!
